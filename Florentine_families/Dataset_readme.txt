The data sets are abstracted form Fig. 2a and 2b (P1276-1277) in "Citation: John F. Padgett, Christopher K. Ansell (1993) Robust action and the rise of the Medici, 1400-1434", The American Journal of Sociology (RSS).

There are 33 family names and nine types of ties in the social structure
Marriage	directed edges,
Partnership	directed edges,
Trade		undirected edges,
Bank Employment	 directed edges,
Real Estate	 directed edge,
Personal Loan	directed edges,
Friendship	 undirected edges,
Patronage	 directed edges,
Mallevadori	 directed edges, 
where green=Personal_Loan + Mallevadori + Partnership + Trade + Bank_Employment + Real_Estate;
red=Marriage + Friendship + Patronage;

There are a few extral edges added based on PADGETT--FLORENTINE FAMILIES dataset. (Two 16¡Á16 matrices).
